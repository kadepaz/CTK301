Kalia de Paz is currently enrolled as a freshman at Illinois State University and is studying Interdisciplinary Creative Technologies. Originally from West Chicago, Illinois, Kalia takes a lot of artistic inspiration from the culturally rich community she grew up in, from art to fashion and even food. She is set to graduate in 2027 and is excited to continue her journey through the design world, learning new skills and finding new interests.
